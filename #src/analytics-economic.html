ECONOMIC CALENDAR

Economic calendar helps traders to keep track of important financial announcements that may affect underlying economies and create price movements. All kind of traders should pay close attention to global events, as such economic calendars are one of the key tools for fundamental analysis.

How to interpret data:

  • If published actual data differs from forecast data, you can expect volatility in the respective currency;

  • A higher difference is likely to cause higher volatility;

  • Impact indicator shows how much volatility is expected.
